Thomas Mazzuchi (he/him), Honors Student

Thomas Mazzuchi (he/him) is a senior double majoring in Mathematics and Psychology. He is interested in how humans generate and interpret speech in context, and is working on an honors project studying the use of pragmatic reasoning in the language games. His dream career is to become a professor of Psychology so that he can share his passion for the subject with others and inspire them to pursue it as well. Outside of the lab, Thomas is the music director of his a cappella group on campus, and plays on the ultimate frisbee team.
